# **MotorPH Payroll System**

(_MS 2 Activity_)

# Description

This project is a payroll system created for our Computer Programming 1 (MO-IT101) course. The program calculates an employee’s weekly salary based on the total hours they worked. The system reads the employee’s log-in and log-out records to compute their working hours. It also considers the company’s office hours and allows a 10-minute grace period for late arrivals.

Government deductions such as SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and withholding tax are originally based on monthly rates. The program converts these deductions into weekly amounts so the employee’s net weekly salary can be calculated.

All results are shown in the program, including employee details, hours worked, gross salary, deductions, and net pay.

## Usage

- **Employee Lookup**
    - Access employee records using individual credentials.
 - **Payroll Staff Access:**
    - Payroll staff can perform administrative lookups with staff credentials.
- **Work Hours Calculation**
    - Track and compute total hours worked based on time logs which also considers the company’s office hours and allows a 10-minute grace period for late arrivals.
    - Includes debugging for accuracy.
- **Gross Salary Computation**
    - Automatically calculates gross pay from hours worked and rates.
- **Government Contributions**
    - Integrated computation for SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and Tax deductions.
    - Ensures compliance with local regulations.
- **Net Salary Deductions**
    - Computes final take-home pay after deductions.
    - Provides detailed breakdown of contributions and taxes.
